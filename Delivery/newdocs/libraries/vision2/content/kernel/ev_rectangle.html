EV_RECTANGLE

EV_RECTANGLE provides a vision2 representation of a rectangle. The rectangle is modeled with the top left coordinate at X, Y with a size of width, height.

Note: You can also query left, right, top and bottom.

Advanced queries are available, including: has, has_x_y and intersects.

Advanced resizing are available, including: include_point, include and merge.
