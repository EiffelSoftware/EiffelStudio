EV_FONT

EV_FONT is a vision2 representation of a computer font available on the native operating system. The following attributes define the characteristics of a vision2 font:

  • Family - The font family, e.g. Family_roman or Family_modern.
  • Weight - The weight of the font, e.g. Weight_regular or Weight_bold.
  • Shape - The shape of the font, e.g.Shape_regular or Shape_italic.
  • Height - The font height in pixels.

As well as performing queries on the attributes listed above, many other queries on the font are supported such as is_proportional or string_size.

Note:If you wish to set a family not available in EV_FONT_CONSTANTS, you can place the name of the family you wish to use in preferred_families, and if a match is found on the native operating system, it will override family
