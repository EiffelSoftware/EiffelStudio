Eiffel ENViSioN! Samples

A number of samples are provided with Eiffel ENViSioN!, designed to illustrate different aspects of the libraries, and how to reuse them in your projects. They are divided into two categories:

  • The Eiffel Class Libraries samples illustrate various aspects of EiffelBase, WEL and EiffelVision 2.
  • The .NET Framework samples focus on demonstrating the use of Eiffel on the .NET Framework.
