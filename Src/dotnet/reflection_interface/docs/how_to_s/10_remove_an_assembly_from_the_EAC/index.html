How to remove an assembly from the Eiffel assembly cache?


  1. To remove an assembly from the Eiffel assembly cache, your current screen has to be the imported assembly viewer, that is the window that appears when you launch ISE Assembly Manager.
    You are now ready to remove an assembly from the Eiffel assembly cache.

  2. Click on the name of the assembly you want to remove (in the table).

  3. Then you have the choice between clicking on the toolbar button and selecting the menu item `Remove' in the `Tools' menu. In both cases the remove dialog will appear and help you remove the assembly.
