Deleting new metrics

To delete a useless metric, you must have already clicked on in the "Metric" tab of the context tool.

  1. Select a metric
  2. Click on the "Delete" button.
  3. Click on "Save" to make deletion effective.
  4. Click on "Exit" to hide the interface.

Caution: After you have saved, metric is definitely lost. If you have clicked on "delete" by mistake, do not save changes!
