EV_SCREEN

EV_SCREEN provides a vision2 representation of the screen. You cannot directly modify the screen properties from within Vision2, but may query the width, height, pointer_position and widget_at_position.

Features are provided for simulating user actions: fake_pointer_button_press, fake_pointer_button_release, fake_pointer_button_click, fake_key_press, fake_key_release and fake_key_click.
