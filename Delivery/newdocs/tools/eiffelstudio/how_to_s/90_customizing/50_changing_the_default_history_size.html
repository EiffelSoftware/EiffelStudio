Changing the default history size

By default, there may not be more than 10 items in the history of a development window. It means that combo boxes in the address bar will let you choose between at most 10 possibilities. You can change this number.

To do so, use the preference window: select Preferences in the Tools menu. Then in the preferences tree, select the "Browsing tools" sub-category in the "Tools" category. On the right part of the window, a list of preferences will appear, including "number of items displayed in the history combo boxes". Click on this item and enter the new value.

Click on save and exit to complete the change.
