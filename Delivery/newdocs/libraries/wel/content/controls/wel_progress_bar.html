WEL_PROGRESS_BAR

A WEL_PROGRESS_BAR control is an encapsulation of the Win32 progress bar. It can be used to display the progress of a lengthy operation.

Note: By specifying the style Pbs_vertical, the progress bar will be displayed vertically.
